    What Are Partial Dentures?

    Partial dentures are removable dental appliances used to replace missing teeth. They are ideal for patients who have lost one or more teeth but still retain some of their natural teeth. A well-fitted partial denture not only restores aesthetics but also improves chewing and speaking ability.

Who Needs Partial Dentures?

    You may be a candidate for partial dentures if you:

  • Have one or more missing teeth

  • Want a non-invasive alternative to implants

  • Are looking for a more affordable option than bridges

  • Need a removable option for easy maintenance
